Stay Safe Online — Checklist
Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone.
Verify links before clicking on emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.
Avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.
Keep apps and devices updated for security.
Be cautious of fake offers, internships, or prize messages.
Log out from shared devices after use.
Limit personal information shared on social media.

UPI Safety
Double-check UPI IDs before sending money.
Never approve unknown payment requests or scan random QR codes.
